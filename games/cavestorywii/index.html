~ About ~

Cave Story is a game released in 2004 by Daisuke Amaya, better known as Studio Pixel.
In 2019, CuckyDev ported CSE2 to the Wii, a decompilation of Cave Story's freeware version.

This is an updated version of the port, with slight changes (such as a pause menu, and certain files being external to allow semi modding support) to make the experience better.
