~ About ~

Jenka's Nightmare is a mod developed by ZarroTsu,
a finished product of a version of the mod Shmitz had originally made.
This is a port of it to CSE2, which allows for a Smooth Scrolling camera, FPS switching,
and rebindable keys.

There is also a port of this mod to the Nintendo Wii! Which you can download for your homebrew needs.
